Problem: windows laptop wired stereo headphones not really stereo
Fix:
in win10 search enter "sound"
click "sound settings"
(plug in headphones)
for "choose your output device" choose the headphones
then choose "device properties"
then choose "additional device properties"
then choose "advanced" tab
unclick "enable audio enhancements"
now sound should be true stereo on headphones
test with youtube "Left and Right Stereo Sound Test"
